Function

The function of a chair is its purpose and practical use. At its core, every chair is designed to provide a stable and comfortable place to sit, but different functions adapt to specific needs. The strength of the legs, the support of the backrest, and the comfort of the seat are all essential parts of how well the chair fulfills its function.
